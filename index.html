About Me

Mr. Wright is an entrepreneur. Throughout his professional career both as the owner of a small businesses and previous thereto, Mr. Wright’s success can be attributed to his persistent innovation and attention to detail. Mr. Wright’s perspectives and approaches to problem solving, organization development and executive service are honed from his experiences as a director of business relations, commercial banker, relationship manager, data specialist, an organizer, strategic communicator and program manager. Mr. Wright’s entrepreneurial career is highlighted by the integrated use of technology for research and communications; providing industry-specific financial insight with emphasis on investment opportunities and related risks in U.S. markets, as well as Europe and Eurasia, the Middle East and North Africa using traditional and new media engagement techniques; and the use of quantitative measures to inform, develop and hone strategic planning and tactical execution.


Mr. Wright most recently applied these capabilities to support investment and risk management operations for hedge funds, institutional traders and multinational corporations. Mr. Wright’s work as the Director of Business Relations at ConStrat and Sidar Global Advisors respectfully, helped the organization develop cutting edge technologies and processes to exploit Big Data environments for the purpose of developing actionable intelligence that guided clients’ decision-making processes. Mr. Wright’s quantitative and qualitative understanding of these environments enabled him to create and provide technical analysis products such as the Social Instability Index and the Retail Investor confidence Index; fundamental analysis products that provide insight from geo-political matters that impact global markets; and complex strategic communications planning services. Mr. Wright’s enterprise- Solomon Group LLC have operated worldwide in the areas of finance, banking, international development, national security and education.


Keys to Mr. Wright’s success include his leadership and interpersonal capabilities. Mr. Wright has built turnkey operations managing the efforts of small project teams of just a couple subject matter experts up to massive full-fledged outreach. In all instances, hands-on interaction with both team leaders and staff were critical to the success of his clients. Mr. Wright’s has been recognized for his professional success in a number of ways. From 2011-2013, his work was reward when his client, ConStrat, was awarded recognition in Inc. Magazine’s 500/5000 Fastest Growing Companies. In 2012, the same company was also awarded Inc. Magazine’s “Hire Award” for job creation. Other professional highlights include briefing matters related to Social Media Analysis & Engagement presentations at the Big Data Combine in Miami, FL.
